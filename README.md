SQUESH'S BLACKHOLE SIMULATION - Welcome and thanks for checking this out!

## What's New in v0.6

- **Polar Jets** — Twin relativistic plasma jets now stream from the black hole's
  poles. They're rendered inside the same ray-tracing pipeline as everything else,
  so they bend, glow, and lens like the real thing. Adjustable via a slider, and
  switched on by default in the M87* and Cygnus X-1 scenarios.

- **Observer Mode** — Experience the black hole from the inside out:
  - *Dive toward the horizon* — the camera plunges inward, the shadow swells to
    swallow your view, and the screen fades to black as you reach the event horizon
    before pulling back out.
  - *Relativistic speed* — fly at up to 0.95c and watch the entire sky aberrate and
    Doppler-shift, bunching and blue-shifting ahead of you: the relativistic
    "headlight effect."

- **Save & Share** — Capture the current view as a clean PNG (interface excluded)
  with a single click.

  And lots more!
